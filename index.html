Footy Japan is a Tokyo based, sports management and marketing company that runs Leagues, cup competitions and football schools.

Established in 2002, Footy Japan Ltd. is a football marketing company providing a professional infrastructure to facilitate a variety of aspects of football in Japan. From organizing 30 teams in three "Premiership" style leagues (TML - Wahl & Case Tokyo Metropolis League), to ensuring that football edification is available to all (B.F.A. - British Football Academy), Footy Japan is committed to delivering football solutions to an ever-demanding market in Japan.
