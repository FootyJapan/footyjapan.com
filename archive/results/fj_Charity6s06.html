Footy Japan/YC&AC Charity 6-a-side Soccer.
  • When: Tuesday March 21st 2006
  • Time: 9:00am - 5:30pm
  • Where: YC&AC
  • Teams: Teams will register up to 10 players. On-field will use 6 players incl GK. Other 4 players may sub on and off multiple times
  • Format: League followed by knockout matches. (To be determined)
  • Cost: Team registration will be ¥25,000 per team.
  • Charity: Through sponsorship, this tournament will be used to raise money for UNICEF. Read about last year here
  • Sponsors: We are looking for corporate sponsors for this event. more details.
  • Registration Period: Registration will end March 13th, to register, please CONTACT US.
  • Special Rules: 3 pitches (50m X 30m) will be set up, across the normal pitch. Regular FIFA rules will be played but no off-sides. Games will be 8 mins e/ w with Finals of 10 mins e/w.
  • Teams entering to date: France Masters, France FC, Swiss Kickers, Santos FC, WS Geckoes, BFC, YC&AC, Schoolboy Errors, Kanto Celts, Vagabonds, Bloomberg, Hibernian, SUFC Japan, Barcap, CSC Tokyo, CareerCross FitnessJP, Lions FC, Nokia FC, Shane FC, Pokka FC, AFC Wanderers, FC Azule.
