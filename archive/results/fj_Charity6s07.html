Footy Japan/YC&AC Charity 6-a-side Soccer.
  • When: Wednesday March 21st 2007
  • Time: 9:00am - 5:30pm
  • Where: YC&AC
  • Teams: Maximum of 24 teams will register up to 10 players. On-field will use 6 players incl GK. Other 4 players may sub on and off multiple times
  • Format: League followed by knockout matches. (To be determined)
  • Cost: Team registration will be ¥25,000 per team.
  • Charity: Through sponsorship, this tournament will be used to raise money for UNICEF. Read about last year here
  • Sponsors: We are looking for corporate sponsors for this event.
